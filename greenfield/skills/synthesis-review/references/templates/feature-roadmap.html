The histogram is computed by the synthesis-review skill before render: it counts each value of features[].size against the sizingScale (S/M/L/XL) and computes heightPct relative to the largest bucket. A heavy XL-skew suggests the scope is too ambitious for the horizon; a heavy S-skew suggests features may need further decomposition.

Features with no personaIds — these touch every persona or are infrastructure-level. They are listed here so they don't get lost in the persona-keyed sprint walk.

Cross-cutting features (empty personaIds[]) typically include observability, logging, deployment plumbing, internal tooling, and platform-level concerns. They must still be sized and sprint-assigned, but they bypass persona-driven prioritization. If this list is empty, the roadmap is fully persona-attributed — flag for confirmation since most non-trivial products have at least one platform-level feature.
